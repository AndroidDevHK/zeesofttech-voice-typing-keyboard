App Title: Voice Typing Keyboard
=========================================

Full Description:
-----------------------------------------
Designed for professionals, students, and travelers, our app transforms your voice into accurate text in real-time. Whether you need to send a quick message, draft a long email, or translate a conversation, our voice keyboard makes it effortless.

Experience the future of mobile input with our premium Glassmorphism UI—a sleek, modern, and translucent design that makes typing a visual delight.

✨ Transform the Way You Type
Stop struggling with small keys. With Voice Typing Keyboard, you can simply speak naturally, and our powerful AI dictation engine will handle the rest. It is the perfect hands-free typing tool for when you are on the move, cooking, or multitasking.

🚀 Key Features You’ll Love:

* Smart Speech to Text: Powered by the latest recognition technology, our talk to text feature understands different accents and languages with incredible precision.

* Offline Translation Mode: Travel the world with confidence. Translate between languages even without an internet connection. Your offline translator is always ready.

* Pro Audio Recorder: More than just a keyboard! Use the built-in high-quality sound recorder to capture lectures, meetings, and voice notes.

* Modern Glassmorphism UI: Upgrade your phone's look with our elegant glass keyboard themes. It’s the most stylish and premium interface on the Play Store.

* Voice Search Anywhere: Quickly search on Reddit, Amazon, YouTube, and Google using just your voice. No more manual typing in search bars!

* Advanced Text Editing: Includes dedicated Undo/Redo buttons and a unique touch-zoom feature to help you edit long texts with ease.

We believe your voice is personal. Our offline mode ensures that your translations and notes stay on your device. We use secure on-device processing to provide a fast, secure, and private vocal typing experience.

How to get started:
1. Enable Voice Typing Keyboard in your Settings.
2. Select it as your default keyboard.
3. Tap the microphone icon and start speaking!
